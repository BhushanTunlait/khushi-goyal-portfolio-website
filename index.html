About Me

I am Khushi Goyal, a dedicated final-year LL.B. (Hons.) student at Acropolis Institute of Law, Indore, with a Bachelor of Commerce from Jiwaji University, Gwalior. My interests lie in Constitutional Law, Human Rights, and Criminal Law.
